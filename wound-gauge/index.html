Wound Gauge

What AI Overviews Do to External Meaning — Standing Measurement Cadence

The Wound Gauge is a standing weekly measurement tracking how AI composition layers (Google AI Overview, Brave Search, ChatGPT search, Perplexity, and others) treat the provenance, attribution, and semantic integrity of external sources. It uses five metrics from the Self-Audit Module for Public Summarizers:

PERProvenance Erasure Rate — how often source identity is stripped
QFSQuery Fidelity Score — how strictly the output adheres to the query
DSLDropped Source Loss — citations omitted while using concepts
ΩErasure Skew — the system praising itself while erasing its sources
SASSource Attribution Score — correct canonical bindings vs. total assertions
